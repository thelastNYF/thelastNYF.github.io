About

Hi! I am a second-year Master student at NLPGM Lab @ Wuhan University, under the supervision of Prof. Tieyun Qian. I obtained my B.Eng. with an honor from Wuhan University in 2024.

My research focuses on the privacy and security of AI reasoning systems, including question answering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and large language models (LLMs).
